Information

Fireballs are defined by NASA as "exceptionally bright meteors that are spectacular enough to be seen over a very wide area." FireBuddy aims to translate complex fireball data sets from NASA's Jet Propulsion Lab into understandable models and visualizations for astronomists, researchers, students, and fireball enthusiasts.

Team Fireball is a group from Professor Rudd's Capstone course at UNC Charlotte and our goal is to create the best web application for presenting NASA's Fireball data.
